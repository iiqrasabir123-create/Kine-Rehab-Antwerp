What we treat

Targeted care for
every kind of pain.

From acute sports injuries to chronic conditions — we build a treatment plan around how your body actually moves.

Sports Rehabilitation

Evidence-based recovery for sprains, muscle tears, joint injuries, and overuse conditions — helping athletes return stronger.

Chronic Pain Relief

Long-term management of back pain, neck pain, arthritis, and fibromyalgia with a blend of manual and therapeutic techniques.

Post-Surgical Recovery

Structured rehabilitation following joint replacements, ligament repairs, spinal surgeries, and other procedures.

Postural & Ergonomics

Assessment and correction of poor posture, workplace-related strain, and repetitive stress injuries from desk or manual work.

Dry Needling

Targeted myofascial release using fine needles to deactivate trigger points, reduce tension, and improve muscle function.

Neurological Rehab

Specialised programmes for patients recovering from stroke, Parkinson's disease, multiple sclerosis, and vestibular disorders.
